About Me

I am the youngest of 5, which includes my two half brothers. I've always gravitated to technology since I was a child, the biggest medium being video games. Naturally, like any respectable gamer, I've dreamed of making my own game for a long time now. I just happened to front-load my fun before the work. Even so, I hope to take at least one more trip, to see my sisters in California.
